Ingredients

  • Garlicky Tomato Sauce: 1 can of tomato sauce and 4 garlic cloves (small-medium)
  • Fresh Mozzarella: 1 or 2 packs of mozarella
  • Pizza dough: store bought dough
  • Fresh herbs: 2 teaspoons of Italian spices
  • Cheese: rasped cheese to liking
  • Truffle oil: Optional addition, brush the crust with oil to increase the aromas

Steps

by James Delmage

  1. Start by placing the dough in a glass bowl and drizzling 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil on top of it.
  2. Let the dough warm up and start working on the sauce.
  3. Chop 4 cloves of garlic and saute on medium heat in 2 Tablespoons of olive oil for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Next, add the full 6 ounces of tomato paste to the pan and 2 cans of water. Be careful with the splattering when the water hits the oil and paste.
  5. Next add the salt and pepper and give the sauce a nice stir. Cover the sauce and turn the heat to medium- low to let it simmer.
  6. Turn on the oven to 450 and place rack in the middle.
  7. Take the rolled out pizza dough and place it on parchment paper. Form the crust with your hands.
  8. Party time! Place all of your toppings and put the pizza in the oven for 10 minutes!
